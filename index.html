‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account deletion take? ‚ñº

Account deletion typically takes 3-30 days from the date we verify your request. Complex requests (with active disputes or legal holds) may take longer. We'll notify you when the process is complete.

Can I undo account deletion? ‚ñº

Once your account is deleted, we cannot restore it. All personal data is permanently removed. If you change your mind, you can create a new account anytime.

Will my trip history be deleted? ‚ñº

Trip history may be retained for up to 12 months for safety and dispute resolution purposes. After 12 months, it will be automatically deleted. You can request immediate deletion if there are no active disputes.

What about payment and invoice records? ‚ñº

Invoice and payment records are retained for 7 years due to tax and accounting compliance requirements. We cannot delete these records by law, but they are stored securely and separately from your personal account data.

Is account deletion the same as deactivation? ‚ñº

No. Deactivation temporarily disables your account (you can reactivate it), while deletion permanently removes all your data. Deletion is a final action.

What if I have an active dispute or open issue? ‚ñº

If you have an active dispute, legal hold, or unresolved issue, we may delay deletion until the matter is resolved. We'll notify you of any holds on your deletion request.

Will deletion affect other users? ‚ñº

Your deletion won't negatively affect other users. However, if you're a parent with an active subscription, your child's rides will be reassigned or cancelled according to the subscription terms.

How do I verify my identity for deletion? ‚ñº

We verify your request by matching the email address and account details you provide. For sensitive cases, we may request additional verification. We'll guide you through the process.

What about children's data (for parent accounts)? ‚ñº

When you delete your parent account, your children's associated data is also deleted (trip subscriptions, assignments, etc.). However, you must first remove them from all active rides.
